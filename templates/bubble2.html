Likes vs. Dislike Ratio

This graph shows the number of likes and dislikes per category by country. The x-axis represents the number of likes and y-axis the number of dislikes. The goal of the this visualization is to present the "likeability" of each category. The way to look on this graph is divide it into 4 quadrants, the bottom right side is the most 'Likeable' followed by the bottom left, then top right and top left is the least likeable. The radius of the bubbles represents the ratio of the Likes and Dislikes of that category. The bigger the bubble the more favorable it is.
